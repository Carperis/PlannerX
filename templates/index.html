    • How to Use?
    Step 1: Login your acount

    To access the login page, click on the "Login" button located in the top-right corner. Enter your username and password to log in. In case you do not have an account yet, click on the "Create an Account!" link to register for a new account. After registration, return to the login page to complete the login process.

    Step 2: Create a plan in dashboard

    After logging in, click on the avatar in the top-right corner of the homepage, and select "Dashboard" to enter your backend. Click on "+ Add Plan" button to start planning your new semester classes.

    Step 3: Enter your classes and preferences

    Using the course planning tool, follow the prompts and instructions to enter your plan name, semester, classes, and preferences. Once you have completed all the necessary fields, click on the "Submit / Update" button to save your plan.

    Step 4: Get, rank, and show your schedules

    Once you have submitted your plan, click on "Get Plans" to calculate all possible schedules. Once the message "Got your plans!" appears, click on "Show Plans" to see your schedules displayed at the bottom of the page. If you wish to find a schedule that best suits your preferences, click on "Rank Plans" to rank your schedules based on your preferences. After ranking your schedules, click "Show Plans" again to view the updated schedules. The first schedule will be the one that best fits your preferences.
